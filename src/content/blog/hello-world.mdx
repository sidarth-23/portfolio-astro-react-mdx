## Why a rebuild?

The previous portfolio was powered by a headless CMS. It worked well, but over time the infrastructure became heavier than the content. Moving to **Astro + MDX** keeps the stack lean, the build fast, and the authoring experience close to the code.

## What changed

- **Content as code**: Blog posts and projects are now MDX files with frontmatter validated by Zod.
- **Astro Image**: Cover images go through Astro's image pipeline for responsive, optimized output.
- **Minimal UI**: shadcn components are used sparingly — cards, badges, buttons — with a restrained design token set.
- **Strict TypeScript**: Every data path is typed, from content collections to page props.

## What's next

This is the baseline. Over time, more posts and projects will land here. For now, the foundation is clean, typed, and ready to grow.
